Admission

Gallery admission is always free. We believe art should be accessible to everyone in our community.

Accessibility

Our gallery is fully wheelchair accessible with elevator access to all floors. Large print exhibition guides available upon request.

Parking

Free street parking available on weekends. Paid parking garage located one block east on Creative Avenue.

Public Transportation

Accessible via Metro Blue Line (Art District Station) and bus routes 15, 23, and 45.
